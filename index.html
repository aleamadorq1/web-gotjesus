About Our App

"Got Jesus?" is a groundbreaking mobile application designed to bridge the gap between faith and technology. We're passionate about bringing the Word of God to millions of mobile devices around the globe, making spiritual growth and connection to faith as easy as a tap on your screen. With features such as Night Mode and bespoke widgets available in the premium version, users can personalize their experience to suit their spiritual journey. Our mission, empowered by the support of our users and Patrons, is to ensure the wisdom of the Bible is accessible, enabling all to fulfill their Christian duty of spreading the Word. Join us and help illuminate the world with God's Word.

Daily Inspirational Quotes:

Our app delivers carefully selected inspirational quotes to uplift every moment of your day. These nuggets of wisdom are presented in a way that's perfect for screen-shots and can be used as motivational background images for your device.

Stunning Design

"Got Jesus?" combines divine inspiration with top-notch aesthetics. Our beautifully designed interface not only enhances usability but also provides a visually stunning user experience that resonates with the majesty of the divine.

Lockscreen Widget

The bespoke lockscreen widget makes divine inspiration a glance away. It feels like God is communicating directly through your phone, providing guidance and wisdom whenever you unlock your device.

Personalized User Experience

From Night Mode to customizable widgets, we offer tools to tailor your spiritual journey. Make the "Got Jesus?" experience uniquely yours.

Community of Believers

Join our supportive community of believers who are committed to spreading God's Word. By becoming a Patron, you contribute to keeping the wisdom of the Bible accessible to all.

Accessible to All

"Got Jesus?" is committed to spreading the Word of God to everyone. If you're unable to make a payment, you can still access the app and enjoy its core features.
